A Pokémon adventure in your terminal

Explore locations, battle wild Pokémon, and complete your Pokédex—all from the command line.

Features

  • Turn-based battles

    Full type matchups and move sets

  • Explore towns and routes

    Walk between Pallet Town, Viridian Forest, Mt. Moon, and more

  • Live Pokédex tracking

    Every Pokémon you meet gets logged with real PokéAPI data

  • NPCs and quests

    Talk to trainers and professors, pick up quests along the way
